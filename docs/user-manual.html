1. What Is This App?

NGL Accounting Suite is a desktop tool built for logistics accounting. It has three main tools:

📄
Merge Tool

Combine invoices, PODs, and bills of lading into organized PDF packets — one per container.

📧
Invoice Sender

Upload a list of invoices, match them to PDF files, and send them through QuickBooks Online automatically.

👥
Customer Manager

Keep track of customer emails, document requirements, and how they prefer to receive invoices.

🔒
Your files stay on your computer. All PDF merging and Excel processing happens locally. Nothing is uploaded to the internet.

2. Getting Started

How to Open the App

🖥
Desktop App (Recommended)

Double-click the NGL Accounting shortcut on your desktop. Everything starts automatically.

🌐
Browser Version

Open the app/index.html file in Chrome or Edge. Run Start Agent.bat first for QBO features.

What Is the "Agent Server"?

The Agent Server is a helper that runs behind the scenes. It handles connections to QuickBooks Online (QBO) and TMS. Think of it like a robot that clicks buttons in QBO for you.

💡
You don't always need it. The Merge Tool and Customer Manager work without the agent. You only need it for sending invoices and fetching missing documents.

5. Merge Tool — Combining Documents

The Merge Tool combines your invoices, PODs, and bills of lading into organized PDF packets. It has two modes:

★ Auto Mode (most common)

Upload an Excel file + PDF files. The app reads the Excel, figures out which PDFs belong to which containers, and merges them automatically.

Manual Mode

Drag in 2 or more PDFs, put them in order, and merge. No Excel needed — great for quick one-off merges.

6.3 Send Methods Explained

Each customer has a send method set in the Customer Manager. This controls how their invoices are delivered:

Standard QBO Email

Sends all attachments (invoice, POD, BL) together in one email through QuickBooks Online.

Most common
QBO Invoice + POD Email (OEC)

Sends the invoice through QBO, then sends the POD in a separate email. For carriers that need the POD delivered separately.

Portal Upload

Merges invoice and POD into one file and uploads it to the carrier's web portal. No QBO email is sent.

7.2 Editing & Deleting

Editing

Click "Edit" on any customer row. The same form opens with their existing information filled in. Make your changes and click "Update Customer."

Deleting

Click "Delete" on an active customer. This deactivates them — they become Inactive and disappear from the active list. They can be brought back anytime by clicking "Activate."

🛈
Nothing is permanently deleted. "Delete" just hides the customer. Switch the filter to "All Customers" to see inactive ones and reactivate them.

7.3 Import & Export

⬆ Import

Click "Import" to load customers in bulk from an Excel, CSV, or JSON file.

  • Required columns: Code and Name
  • Optional: Email, CC, BCC, Send Method, Required Docs, Notes
  • Column names are matched flexibly
⬇ Export

Click "Export" to download your entire customer list as an Excel file.

  • Good for backups
  • Good for sharing between computers
  • Can be re-imported later

9. Tips & Troubleshooting

Best Practices

💡
  • Always test first — Use "Test Mode" in the Invoice Sender before doing a real send.
  • Keep customer emails updated — Invoices can only be sent if the customer has an email on file.
  • Use "Per Container" merge — It's the most organized format and what most customers expect.
  • Check the Failure Report — After a merge, look at the failure report to see if any PDFs couldn't be matched.
  • Export your customer list — Click "Export" in Customer Manager periodically as a backup.
  • Name PDFs clearly — Include the container number or invoice number in the file name so the app can match them.
  • Save to Folder — Use "Save to Folder" instead of individual downloads for large batches.

Common Problems

ProblemWhat to Do
"Agent Offline" badge The background service isn't running. Close and reopen the app. If using the browser version, run Start Agent.bat.
QBO shows "Not Connected" Go to Settings and click "Re-login." A browser window will open for you to sign into QuickBooks.
Excel columns not recognized Make sure your spreadsheet has columns for Container Number and Invoice Number. The app accepts many name variations, but the column must exist.
PDFs not matching to containers Check that the PDF file names contain the container number or invoice number somewhere in the file name.
Invoice shows "No Email" That customer doesn't have an email on file. Go to Customer Manager, find them, click Edit, and add their email address.
Invoice shows "No Match" The customer isn't in your database. Add them in Customer Manager first, then reload the CSV.
Send failed for an invoice Usually means the QBO session timed out. Go to Settings, re-login to QBO, and retry the failed invoices.
App is slow during merge Normal for large batches (100+ containers). The app processes everything on your computer. Let it finish — you can keep working in other windows.

10. Glossary

Quick definitions of terms used throughout this manual:

TermWhat It Means
QBOQuickBooks Online — the accounting software used to send invoices to customers.
TMSTransportation Management System — used to track shipments and find POD documents.
PODProof of Delivery — a document confirming that goods were delivered to the customer.
BL / BOLBill of Lading — the shipping contract document that travels with the cargo.
Agent ServerA background service on your computer that handles QBO and TMS connections. Think of it as a robot that clicks buttons for you.
CSVComma-Separated Values — a simple spreadsheet format you can export from most programs.
OECA send method where the invoice goes through QBO and the POD is sent in a separate email.
Container NumberThe unique ID stamped on a shipping container (e.g., MSCU1234567). Always 4 letters + 7 numbers.
Drop ZoneThe dashed-border area in the app where you drag and drop files. You can also click it to browse.
Test ModeA setting in Invoice Sender that lets you preview the send process without actually emailing anything.
